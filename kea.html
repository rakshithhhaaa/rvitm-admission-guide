Admission Procedure

KEA Admission

Follow these steps in order at the time of admission.

  1. 1

    Visit Office 2 first

    Begin the admission process by reporting to Office 2.

  2. 2

    Submit your documents

    Submit all original documents along with two Xerox (photocopy) copies of each original document.

  3. 3

    Pay the admission fees

    Pay the admission fees only to the designated college bank account. See the note below.

  4. 4

    Return to Office 2

    Come back to Office 2 after completing the bank payment.

  5. 5

    Photograph capture

    The candidate's photograph will be captured by the college. (KEA admissions only.)

  6. 6

    Two copies generated

    After the photograph is captured, two copies are generated – a Candidate Copy and a College Copy.

  7. 7

    College copy processed

    The College Copy is scanned, uploaded, and attached to the student's admission record.

  8. 8

    VTU online admission entry

    College staff complete the VTU Online Admission Entry by entering student details and uploading the scanned documents to the VTU portal.

  9. 9

    Admission completed

    Admission is completed after successful online verification.

⚠ Important: All admission fees must be paid only to the designated college bank account. Payments made to any other bank account will not be accepted.
