Admission Procedure

KEA Admission

Follow these steps in order at the time of admission.

ⓘ Important notes

  • Parents must provide a valid Email ID and Phone Number
  • Carry all original documents
  • Carry two Xerox (photocopy) copies of each original document
  1. 1

    Visit Office 2

    Report to Office 2 to begin the admission process.

  2. 2

    Submit your documents

    Submit all original documents along with two Xerox (photocopy) copies of each original document.

  3. 3

    Pay the college fees

    Pay the college fees by UPI only.

  4. 4

    Photograph capture

    The candidate's photograph will be captured by the college. (KEA admissions only.)

  5. 5

    Two copies generated

    After the photograph is captured, two copies are generated – a Candidate Copy and a College Copy.

  6. 6

    College copy processed

    The College Copy is scanned, uploaded, and attached to the student's admission record.

  7. 7

    Online data entry

    College staff complete the online data entry by entering student details and uploading the scanned documents to the VTU portal.

⚠ Important: Pay college fees by UPI only.

Documents Required at the Time of Admission — KEA

  1. 1Original of 10th and 12th Marks cards
  2. 2Original Transfer certificate & Study certificate (compulsory)
  3. 3If qualified other than the PU Board of Karnataka: original Transfer certificate & Migration certificate (compulsory)
  4. 4If the seat is allotted under a category: relevant Category / Income / Rural / Kannada Medium / Hyderabad Karnataka / Sports / NCC / Defense / J&K Resident certificate
  5. 5Photocopy of Student Aadhaar card
  6. 6Photocopy of Parent PAN Card & Aadhaar card
  7. 71 recent colour Passport size photograph
  8. 8For students who studied outside India: copy of Passport & Visa
  9. 92 sets of photocopies of all the documents listed above
  10. 10Final Copy, Verification slip, and fees paid details in KEA

Arrange the documents in the above order for a smooth admission process.
