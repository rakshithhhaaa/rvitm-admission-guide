Admission Procedure

Management Admission

Follow these steps in order at the time of admission.

ⓘ Important notes

  • Parents must provide a valid Email ID and Phone Number
  • Carry all original documents
  • Carry two Xerox (photocopy) copies of each original document
  1. 1

    Visit Office 2

    Report to Office 2 to begin the admission process.

  2. 2

    Submit your documents

    Submit all original documents along with two Xerox (photocopy) copies of each original document.

  3. 3

    Pay the college fees

    Pay the college fees by UPI only.

  4. 4

    Online data entry

    College staff complete the online data entry.

⚠ Important: Pay college fees by UPI only.

Documents Required at the Time of Admission — Management

  1. 1Entrance exam rank card of KEA / COMEDK / JEE
  2. 2Original of 10th and 12th Marks cards
  3. 3Original Transfer certificate & Study certificate (compulsory)
  4. 4If qualified other than the PU Board of Karnataka: original Transfer certificate & Migration certificate (compulsory)
  5. 5Photocopy of Student Aadhaar card
  6. 6Photocopy of Parent PAN Card & Aadhaar card
  7. 71 recent colour Passport size photograph
  8. 8For students who studied outside India: copy of Passport & Visa
  9. 92 sets of photocopies of all the documents listed above

Arrange the documents in the above order for a smooth admission process.
