↑ Downloading your fee payment receipt

This procedure applies to KEA, COMEDK and Management admissions.

  1. 1

    Log in to the RVITM website

    Go to www.rvitm.edu.in on your phone or computer browser.

  2. 2

    Open Online Fee Payment

    From the website menu, click on Online Fee Payment.

    Log in with your credentials

    Log in using your Username & Password.

  4. 4

    Generate the receipt and submit it in the admission department.
